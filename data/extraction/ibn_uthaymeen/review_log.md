# Review Log — Ibn Uthaymeen Book

Use this file to track scholarly review decisions page by page.

## Review Workflow
1. Open page record in `clean_pages.json`.
2. Compare with `raw_pages.json` and source markdown.
3. Decide one:
   - `approved`
   - `approved_with_edit`
   - `rejected`
4. Record reason and action.
5. Keep unresolved pages in `pending`.

## Priority Guidance
- **High priority:** pages with doctrinal definitions and low OCR quality.
- **Medium priority:** readable pages with minor OCR noise.
- **Low priority:** front-matter and publication metadata pages.

## Completion Criteria
- All pages reviewed.
- No page remains `pending` for publication batch.
- Rejections are corrected and re-submitted.
